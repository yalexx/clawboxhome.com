Why Your Home Needs Local AI

Cloud AI services like ChatGPT, Copilot, and Google Gemini offer impressive capabilities — but they come with serious trade-offs for home users. Every question you ask is transmitted to remote servers, logged for model training, subject to privacy policy changes, and dependent on internet connectivity.

For home use cases involving personal health information, financial details, family schedules, or private communications, cloud dependency creates real risk. ClawBox eliminates that risk entirely by keeping AI computation within your home network.

The practical advantages are significant:

  • Privacy: Your family’s data never leaves your home router
  • Reliability: Works during internet outages for many tasks
  • Speed: Zero round-trip latency to cloud servers
  • Cost: No per-query fees or escalating subscription costs
  • Control: You decide what the AI can and cannot do
  • Longevity: Hardware you own, software that is open source

As AI becomes embedded in everyday home life — managing appliances, health routines, financial planning, and family communications — having that AI run locally becomes a matter of both privacy and resilience.

Technical Specifications

ClawBox is built around the NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano — the most powerful edge AI processor in its class, delivering desktop-grade AI performance in a compact, energy-efficient package.

  • Processor: NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano 8GB module
  • AI Performance: 67 TOPS (Tera Operations Per Second)
  • RAM: 8GB LPDDR5 (unified CPU+GPU memory)
  • Storage: 512GB NVMe SSD (M.2 2280)
  • GPU: 1024-core NVIDIA Ampere GPU
  • CPU: 6-core ARM Cortex-A78AE
  • Connectivity: Gigabit Ethernet, WiFi 6, Bluetooth 5.0
  • Ports: USB 3.2, USB-C, HDMI 2.1, GPIO
  • Power: 15W typical, 7W minimum mode
  • OS: Ubuntu 22.04 LTS with OpenClaw pre-installed
  • Dimensions: Compact desktop form factor

The Jetson Orin Nano was designed for embedded AI applications requiring constant operation at low power. It is used in industrial robots, autonomous vehicles, and medical devices — ClawBox brings that same reliability to the home.
